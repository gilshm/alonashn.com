Research

My research lies at the intersection of fundamental materials science and soft robotics. I focus on understanding the material properties that influence the performance of high-performance dielectrics for artificial muscles.

I also develop biodegradable materials that meet the electrical and mechanical requirements of artificial muscles while remaining soft, flexible, and strong. In parallel, I design and fabricate soft robotic applications, including haptic feedback devices and assistive tools for tremor suppression.
